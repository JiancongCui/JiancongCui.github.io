About Me
I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Northeastern University (NEU, 2024-), advised by Prof. Kevin Fu. Meanwhile, I would like to extend my sincere appreciation to Yan Long, a Post-doc colleague in our lab, for his invaluable guidance. Before that I received my master's degree from the University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS, 2021-2024) , working under the guidance of Prof. Lei Cui and Prof. Hongsong Zhu. During that period, I was also honored to work with Prof. Yuede Ji, who has provided me with invaluable guidance. Looking further back, the journey of my research began at the Data Science and Technology Lab of Shandong Normal University (2017-2021), under the guidance of Prof. Hong Wang.
